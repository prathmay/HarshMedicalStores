About Us

Harsh Medical Stores has been serving the people of Sendhwa, since 10 July 1993. Founded by Mr Mukesh Kanungo, the store was built on the simple idea of providing the right medicines with honesty and care.

From the outset, the focus has been on trust and reliability. Over the years, families have continued to return, building lasting relationships.

Today, the responsibility continues under Harsh Kanungo, carrying forward the same values with care and dedication.
